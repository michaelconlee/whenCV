Our Accomplishments

  • 2018: Tabled at several DIY artist events in San Francisco, including the Renegade Craft Fair and West Coast Craft, where we sold out of our artist prints and gained recognition for our unique approach to supporting emerging artists.
  • 2019: Hosted a grant-supported art event in New York City showcasing artists' editions, original works, and live music. The event, titled "Momentum," was held in a basement venue in Williamsburg and drew over 500 attendees, cementing WHEN Collective's presence in the New York arts scene.
  • 2020: Published WHEN Magazine issue 1, a 100-page catalog featuring original artwork, writing, and photography. The publication was distributed to over 1,000 individuals and helped raise awareness for our mission of supporting emerging artists and writers.
  • 2021: Hosted a second grant-supported art event in New York City, this time at the Knockdown Center in Maspeth. Titled "Spectrum," the event featured over 30 artists and performers and drew over 1,000 attendees, cementing our reputation as a leading force in the New York arts scene.
  • 2022: Launched WHEN FM, an internet radio station where we mentor college-aged DJs and musicians toward sharing their work and expressing their love for music. The station features a diverse range of programming, from ambient and experimental music to talk shows and interviews, and has quickly gained a loyal following.
